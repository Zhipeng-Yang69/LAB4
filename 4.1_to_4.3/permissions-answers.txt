#Permissions of Groups,I use chown and chmod create a shared group,the owner is FrobozzCo
/shared permission : drwxrwx--- that means members of FrobozzCo can read/write/execute
others have no access.
commands:groupadd FrobozzCo
         useradd -G FrobozzCo joe
         useradd -G FrobozzCo mary
         mkdir /root/share_perm
         chown :FrobozzCo/root/share_perm
         chmod 770 /root/share_perm
         ls -ld /root/share_perm >> /root/permissions-answers.txt
drwxrwx--- 2 root FrobozzCo 4096 Oct  7 03:00 /root/share_perm
/shared permission : drwxrwx--- that means members of FrobozzCo can read/write/execute
